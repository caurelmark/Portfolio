Hobbies

League of Legends

League of Legends is a MOBA or (Multiplayer Online Battle Arena) where many players fight each other using champions and try to destroy the enemies' nexus.


Little Nightmares

Little Nightmares is puzzle-platformer horror adventure game made by Tarsier Studios. It is about a hungry little nine-year old girl named Six trapped in The Maw (A mysterious vessel catering to the whims of sick and powerful creatures.) and want to make her escape.


Farcry 3

Farcry 3 is a FPS-adventure game developed by Ubisoft Montreal, Massive Entertainment, Ubisoft Shanghai, Red Storm Entertainment and Ubisoft Reflections. It is about a group of friends that decided to take a vacation, they start partying and skydiving, but it all changed when they got caught by pirates. What would Jason Brody do? Learn the ways of the forest. Save your friends. Kill Vaas.

Need for Speed: Rivals

Need for Speed: Rivals is a racing video game developed in a collaboration between Ghost Games and Criterion Games and published by EA(Electroic Arts). You take the role of the Racer or Cop, you can switch in between your games. Complete the missions. Take out your Rivals.

Titanfall 2

Titanfall 2 is a science-fiction first-person shooter game and a sequel to Titanfall and developed by Respawn Entertainment. It is about Jack Cooper, a Rifleman-class 3, promoted to being the pilot of BT-7274, together they try to stop the war. They say the bond between the Pilot and the Titan is immeasurable.
